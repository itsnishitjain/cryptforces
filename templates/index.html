A platform that helps people learn cryptic hunting.

Our platform provides resourcesto to individuals and enables organizations to host their own hunts, which involve deciphering codes and clues to solve puzzle-solving challenges.

What do we provide at cryptforces?

At cryptforces, we provide a platform for individuals to learn cryptic hunting with various practice questions in problemset and learning resources. We also help organisations to host their own events with customisation.

  • Cryptic hunting resources

    At cryptforces, we provide a variety of resources to help individuals learn and master the art of cryptic hunting. This includes guides on how to decipher codes and clues, tips on how to solve puzzles, and practice puzzles to help you hone your skills.

  • Organise and host your own hunts

    Our platform allows organisations to host their own hunts with complete customisation so that they dont need to worry about coding their own platform and enhance their community in an easy way.

  • Discuss in Forums

    Cryptic hunting is not just an individual pursuit; it often involves working with a team to solve puzzles and decipher clues. At cryptforces, we encourage collaborative learning by providing forums and discussion boards where hunters can share tips, ask questions, and collaborate on puzzles.
